Topics
Trees
Binary Search Trees

Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, students will be able to:
Explain what a binary tree is.
Explain the organization of the binary tree data structure.
Explain how operations such as search, add, remove, and traverse are performed on a binary tree.
Implement a relatively simple binary tree class.
Explain what a binary search tree (BST) is.
Explain the organization of a BST.
Explain how operations such as search, add, and remove are performed on a BST.
Determine the asymptotic time complexity of search, add, and remove operations on a BST.
